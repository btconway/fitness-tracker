## Key Notes

### Progression Strategy
- **Armor Building:** Stick to the rep ranges; increase load when you can maintain form
- **Pullups/Pushups:** Follow the prescribed sets. These programs build toward max strength (3RM/5RM) and endurance capacity
- **Steps:** Daily 10k step goal—log morning walks before lifting

### Weekly Carries (Mon/Fri)
On **Days 1 (Mon) and 5 (Fri)** of each 7-day cycle, add supplementary KB work after carries:
- **3 sets × 25 KB Swings**
- **3 sets × 10 KB Rows**

### Recovery Weeks
**Weeks 4 (Days 6–7)** are built-in recovery—can be active rest or total off days, but still hit your step count.

### Weekday Counting (Pullup/Pushup)
- Fighter programs count **weekdays only** (Mon–Fri)
- Weekends = automatic rest days
- Use deferral if you miss a weekday

## Sample Training Day

**Example: Mon, Mar 17 (AB Complex + Farmer Carries + Supp KB)**

1. **Warmup:** Hang from bar, deep squat sit, short brisk walk
2. **AB Complex (4–5 rounds):**
   - 2 Double KB Cleans
   - 1 Double KB Press
   - 3 Front Squats
3. **Farmer Carries:** 3 × 40 yards
4. **Supplementary KB Work:**
   - 3 sets × 25 KB Swings
   - 3 sets × 10 KB Rows
5. **Fighter Pullup Program** (weekday #~17 of 5RM): e.g., 7, 6, 5, 4, 3 total reps
6. **Fighter Pushup Program** (weekday #~17 of Phase 2): e.g., 25, 24, 22, 20, 16 total reps
7. **Cooldown:** 15–30 min walk
8. **Daily Goal:** 10,000 steps
